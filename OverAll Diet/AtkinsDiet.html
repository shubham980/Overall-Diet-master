What is Atkins Diet?

Background:

Carbohydrates intake produce insulin hormone inside the body. This insulin inturn leads to cravling inside the human body like hunger for carbohydrates which inturns leads to more weight gain and obesity.

Description:

The Atkins diet is a diet made specifically for weightLoss by reducing the carbohydrates to a minimal level.The diet consists of high protiens , fats and minimal carbohydrates. Fats are used in the diet to get the energy in place of carbohydrates.

Benefits Of Atkins Diet

The diet is benefical for obese people and women with PCOS syndrome where loosing weight in essential in order to cure diseases. Since insulin production would be low, people would tend to have less craving and hence maintain or decrase the weight accordlingly.

Things to mind!

Atkins Diet does comes with risk factors too:

  • No carbs can lead to weakness which in turns leads a person become more irritable
  • No carbs means less energy for exercising.
  • Sometimes you may not feel full or even feel bloated which can cause sleeping problems and constipation problems too.
