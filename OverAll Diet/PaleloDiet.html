What is Palelo Diet?

Background:

Taking back to the medival times when there were no processed food and human used to feed on the raw materials like raw fruits and vegetable. The Palelo diet forms it origins from this very basic idea that if in those day humans were able to digest that food which was purely clean with any argicultural process and clinical treatment at all , in today world these food should prove healthy and should be of a greater benefits than the processed food we intake.

Description:

The paeleo diet main aim is reduce the amount of processed and canned food and keep a simple diet regime mostly inclusing very basic food.Paleo Diet has gone throught different versions of modication and now-a-days there is a growing trend of gluten free food which can be considered a part of the palelo diet.
