What is Vegan Diet?

Background:

Sometime meat and fish are not easy to digest since they are made of complex compounds or might not suit a certain individuals and cause issue with their digestive tracks.

Description:

Vegan Diet is as simple as it name indicates i.e. eating a lots of fruits and veggies which provide the necessary nutrients as well as fibres.

Benefits Of Vegan Diet

Great for people who want to turn vegan, eat healthy or loose weight as the same time.Also this diet is rich in fibre which means its improves the digestion capabilities of person. No pressure on kidney to digest complex foods hence improves the functioning of digestive organs,

Things to mind!

Vegan Diet does comes with risk factors too:

  • Limited options available
  • Protein content in vegetration is less which can hamper in the way of weight loss
