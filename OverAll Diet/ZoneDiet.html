What is Zone Diet?

Background:

Zone Diet was developed by Dr Barry Sears.It was initially developed for people with cardiovascular diseases. The basic goal for developing zone diet was weight loss since weight gain and obesity are the major fundamental causes of people with cardiovasular diseases and in turn obesity increases the chances of worsening these conditions.

Description:

The primary goal of the zone diet is to reduce inflamation which is achived by cutting of the major carbohydrates and taking in healthy insulins with a signiciant amount of Omega-3 involved in the diet process.This diet has a requirement of 8 ounces of water daily. The water content helps to remove all the toxins built in inside the body thus helping in reducing the chances of inflamation.

Benefits Of Zone Diet

The primary benefits of the zone is to reduce inflamation of all types from the body ranging from gastric inflamation to sereve inflamation
Except from the primary aim there are additional benefits of the zone diet.

  • Since Zone diet have fixed portion that a person has to eat, the zone diet encourges balanced diet habits
  • This diet is rich in fibre content since most of the intake is fruits and vegetables.
  • Since the intake of carbohydrates is next to minimal, craving for unhealthy intake reduce to a great extent

Things to mind!

There are no major risk associted with zone diet but only a few things to keep in mind:

  • Zone diet can be advantageous to vegans since it involves a high portion intakes of veggies
  • Fat are a big no , only a tiny bit in every meal will do
  • Zone diet can be suitable for with diabetes also, but the doctors advice is mandatory
  • Any form of exercise is mandatory if weight loss is goal.
  • One needs to follow precisely the portions i.e one-third proteins and two-third portion of carbohydrates and a little bit of fat
