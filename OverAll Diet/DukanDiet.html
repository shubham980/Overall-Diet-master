What is Dukan Diet?

Background:

Proteins are the basic body building units of the body.While carbohydrates provide energy they also add to obesity in the body. Protiens are known to repair the cell faster and also keep the human body fuller for a longer duration of time.

Description:

The main goal of dukan diet is to increase the protien intake in diet of the person since protein are known to cut down the stored body fat inside our bodies. Dukan diet can also be considered as a pure protien diet with main focus on weight loss.The diet is divided into different section starting from section one where the person is allowed to take lean proteins followed by addition of veggies in the protein diet and then taking a mixture of both unlimited but balanced.

Benefits Of Dukan Diet

The main benefit of the dukan diet is that in the later stages of the diet, one can eat as many as protein rich food as he wishes. The second thing is that in this diet, initially a significant result is noticeable thus encouraging the people.

Things to mind!
